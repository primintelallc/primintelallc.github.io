Privacy Policy

PRIMINTELA LLC · Last updated July 4, 2026

This policy covers all games published by PRIMINTELA LLC, including Ant Farm. Our games are made for families, and our approach to privacy is simple: we don't collect your data.

What we collect

Nothing. Our games:

  • Do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information
  • Do not require an account or sign-in
  • Do not use third-party analytics

Where game progress lives

All game progress (player profiles, collected ants, coins, and settings) is stored locally on your device. If your device backs itself up (for example to iCloud or Google's device backup), your game progress is included in that backup under your own account — we never see it.

Children's privacy

Our games are designed for children and comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) the simplest way possible: no data about anyone — child or adult — is collected at all.

Changes to this policy

If a future game or update ever changes any of the above, we will update this page before releasing it and note the change here.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Email us at contact@primintela.org.
